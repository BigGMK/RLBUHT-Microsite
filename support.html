Support

Confused about why your computer is not behaving as it should or need to know more about how to work a specific systems such as Lync or PACS? The support section aims to answer the most frequently asked IT questions to enable you to solve your problem as quickly and efficiently as possible.

In addition, the 'how to guides' and videos will provide you with step by step instructions on how to use the Royal's IT systems to ensure they support you in the best possible way and you get the very most out of each solution. Of course, this does not take the place of the support desk but it should be your first port of call to quickly and easily find the answer you are looking for.
